What is this?

Actually, this is a simple timer.
With some neat features.
And this features, help you to boost your productivity.
(At least it has improved mine)

A lot of blog posts recommend to work for
a specific amount of time, then relax a little bit:
45 minutes work and 30 minutes rest.
48 minutes work and 12 minutes rest.
90 minutes work and 30 minutes rest.
...

What can I do?

With this tool, you can specify three things:

First: The time you want to work.
Second: The time you want to relax.
Third: How often you want to repeat this cycle.
